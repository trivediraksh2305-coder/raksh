Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get a service professional?
In most cases, we can schedule a service professional within 24-48 hours. For emergency services, we offer same-day service in many areas.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve over 25 major cities across the country. Enter your zip code during booking to check availability in your area.
Are your service professionals background checked?
Yes, all our service professionals undergo thorough background checks, verification, and skill assessments before joining our platform.
What if I need to reschedule or cancel?
You can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled service time at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and bank transfers.
